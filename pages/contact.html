Reporting data issues

If you find an error in the data — incorrect prices, missing bars, bad adjustments — please email me with:

  • Ticker symbol
  • Date(s) affected
  • Description of the issue
  • How you identified it (comparison source, expected value, etc.)

All reported issues will be investigated and documented in the Known Issues log with credit to the reporter.

Requesting new tickers

The current universe covers 1,391 U.S. equities and ETFs. If you need a ticker that isn't included, email me the symbol and the reason for the request. I will add tickers in batches as sources permit.
