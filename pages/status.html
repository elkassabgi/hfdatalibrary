How to read this page. current   one trading day behind (usually an upstream exchange-feed delay — the service is fine, and the pipeline self-heals the gap on its next run)   two or more trading days behind, or the API is down. Data for a trading day is published the following morning (~08:00 UTC), so "current" means through the last completed trading day as of the most recent pipeline run.
